Lasagna

Pasta Sheets, Cheese, Tomato Sauce, Beef

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made of wide, flat pasta sheets layered with rich fillings like ground meat (often beef or pork), tomato sauce, and creamy b√©chamel or ricotta cheese.

The layers are repeated, creating a stacked structure, with the top usually covered in melted cheese, typically mozzarella or parmesan. Baked until golden and bubbling, lasagna offers a hearty, comforting combination of flavors and textures, with variations including vegetarian options and different regional takes across Italy and around the world.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Pasta Sheets
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Beef

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Add Pasta Sheets to bottom of pan
  3. Add layers of ingedients, in order of: Pasta, Tomato Sauce, Cheese, Beef. Repeat until pan is full
  4. Bake for 70 minutes or until cheese is fully melted and golden brown
