Tacos

Tortilla of your choice, Pico de gallo, Chicken, beef or Fish

Description

Tacos are a popular Mexican dish consisting of a soft or crispy tortilla filled with a variety of ingredients. Traditional fillings often include seasoned meats like beef, pork, or chicken, but they can also feature fish, beans, or vegetables for a vegetarian option.

Tacos are typically garnished with fresh toppings such as onions, cilantro, salsa, and lime, offering a flavorful and customizable experience. Served as street food or a meal, tacos are beloved for their simplicity, versatility, and the vibrant combination of textures and tastes.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Pico de gallo mix
  • Protein of your choice

Steps

  1. Cook protein
  2. Add protein sauce to Tortilla
  3. Add Pico de gallo mix to Tortilla
  4. Enjoy your Tacos!
